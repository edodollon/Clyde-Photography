Clyde Capistrano

Clyde Capistrano is a Filmmaker and Photographer based in Vancouver, BC Canada. He wrote and directed “Goodnight, Bella,” an award winning short film that premiered in various film festivals around the world. He is also the founder of Above and Beyond Productions, a wedding photo and video company that is also based in Vancouver. They aim to help clients capture every special moment and distil it to its rawest form, to be enjoyed forever.
